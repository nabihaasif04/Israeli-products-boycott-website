About Our Mission

Falasطيني empowers conscientious consumers to make informed purchasing decisions that align with their values. Our platform provides comprehensive information about companies and products to boycott in solidarity with Palestine.

By redirecting our collective spending power away from corporations that support oppression, we can create meaningful change through non-violent means. Together, we're building a movement of ethical consumerism that stands for justice and human rights.
